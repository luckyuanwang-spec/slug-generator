💡 SEO Tips for URL Slugs

  • Keep slugs short and descriptive (under 50 characters)
  • Include your main keyword in the slug
  • Use hyphens (-) to separate words, never underscores
  • Remove unnecessary words like articles and prepositions
  • Avoid special characters and spaces

"How to Make Coffee at Home?" → how-to-make-coffee-home

"Easy & Quick Recipes: Pasta 🍝" → easy-quick-recipes-pasta

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL slug?
A URL slug is the part of a URL that identifies a specific page in a human-readable way.
Why are slugs important for SEO?
SEO-friendly slugs help search engines understand your page content.
Should I use hyphens or underscores in slugs?
Always use hyphens (-) in your slugs.
How long should a slug be?
Ideally, keep your slugs under 50 characters.
Can I change my slug after publishing?
Yes, but set up a 301 redirect if you change it.
